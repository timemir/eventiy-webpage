You and your city

Your city is an integral part of the Eventify ecosystem. Here you can find not only the events of the residents, but also the events of the city. Never miss one of the beautiful city festivals again!

Look around on an inactive map and see all the events near you. So you'll always be up to date and know where you want to go next.

Stay in touch with all event participants with the help of a chat. This makes planning easy and effective.
